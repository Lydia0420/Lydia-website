UPKP Logo Design

First Draft

This company specializes in medical device after-sales and repair services. As a supporter of the medical field, I chose the word upkeep, forming UPKP.

Second Draft

The second draft presents refined variations of the logo, incorporating design improvements based on feedback. These designs focus on clarity, symbolism, and brand identity.

Third Draft (3.1 Version)

Based on the final selection from the second draft, this version explores six refined iterations, testing visual balance, scalability, and symbol integration.

Third Draft (3.2 Color Variations)

Color variations of the final logo concept. Some of these designs resemble the colors of the four seasons, potentially allowing for branding flexibility across different contexts.

Third Draft (3.3 Refinement)

Final refinements to the logo's proportions, spacing, and overall balance. Each variation is carefully adjusted to enhance clarity and branding consistency.

Adjusted letter spacing for better readability.

Leaf icon slightly enlarged for emphasis.

Refined alignment between text and icon.

More contrast between text and logo.

Logo icon isolated for standalone branding.

Modified proportions for balance.

Experimenting with stacked logo layout.

Optimized for small-scale applications.

Final Version

The final UPKP logo is designed in two versions: a standard version and a reversed color version, ensuring adaptability in different branding applications.
