Why am I so tired at twenty?

The kind of burnout doesn’t come from overwork, but from the pressure to become someone better, faster, more likable, more seen. This film explores how productivity culture becomes internalized as selfblame and invisible pressure.

Final Documentary

This short documentary explores how a generation raised on optimization, digital validation and endless comparison is experiencing emotional burnout and self doubt before life has even begun. Through personal reflections, visual metaphors and interviews with young people in their early twenties, the film examines how social structures like productivity culture have been internalized as self blame and invisible pressure. I want to understand how “not being enough” has become a shared cultural condition, especially for young adults coming of age in a hyper mediated world. Like The Social Dilemma and La Jetée, convey the inner landscape of a generation caught between idealized futures and the disenchanted now.
